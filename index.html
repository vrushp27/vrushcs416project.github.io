MPG For Cars (YEAR: 2017)

Author: Vrush Patel

In today's world, many people buy cars without checking the mileage. Most of the young population nowadays ignores the fact that gas is super expensive as well as it's polluting the environment. Slowly, the entire world is leaning towards buying electric vehicles; however, many people still think they're expensive and not worth the price; however, the don't think that they will end up spending so much money in gas over many years if they keep that low MPG car. This Narrative Visualization will further explain different charts in how you can save a lot of money in buying specific brands of cars that give good MPG as well as leaning them towards getting an electric car which will be better for the environment.
